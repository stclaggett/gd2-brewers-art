100 Ways
The 100 ways project is at once a biography, a dissection, an exploration, and a jumping off point. Each student chooses an object and attempts to depict it 100 different ways with the greatest amount of variation possible. Through this process, students are forced to investigate both the qualities that make their object unique and the range of methods for depicting their object.
